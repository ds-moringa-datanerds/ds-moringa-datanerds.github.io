<h1>❓ Frequently Asked Questions</h1>
<p>Can't find your answer here? Ask on <strong>Mattermost #general</strong> or check <strong>Canvas announcements</strong>.</p>

<div class="faq-category">Programme & Schedule</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">How long is the programme?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">The part-time programme runs for 37 weeks. It consists of 6 modules that progressively build your data science skills from Python basics through advanced machine learning.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">What's the weekly time commitment?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">Plan for scheduled class sessions plus 5–10 hours of independent study, practice, and assignments per week. The exact schedule will be shared at the start of each module.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">What if I miss a class?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">Attendance is tracked and matters for your progress. If you miss a session, check Canvas for the materials and the Mattermost #announcements channel for the post-session summary. Reach out to your instructors if you need help catching up.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">How is the course assessed?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">Module Labs (25%), Module Quizzes (5%), Summative Labs (45%), Summative Quizzes (25%). There are also 3 capstone projects at the end of the course.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-category">Technical Setup</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">What laptop specs do I need?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">Minimum: Core i5 processor or above, 8GB RAM, 500GB storage, and stable internet access. This is sufficient for all the tools and libraries we'll use throughout the programme.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">How do I install Python?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">We recommend installing Python through Anaconda, which bundles Python with all the data science libraries you'll need. Visit the <a href="/resources/">Resources page</a> for step-by-step setup instructions.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">Do I need prior programming experience?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">No! Module 1 starts from the very basics of programming. We'll build your skills progressively. What you do need is curiosity, willingness to practice, and comfort with basic math and logic.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">What is CodeGrade and how do I use it?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">CodeGrade is the platform where you submit coding assignments and receive automated feedback. It integrates with Canvas — you'll access it directly from your assignment pages. See the <a href="https://help.codegrade.com/for-students/getting-started/getting-started-in-canvas">CodeGrade getting started guide</a>.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-category">Getting Help</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">Where do I ask technical questions?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">Post in the <strong>Mattermost #module-help</strong> channel. Include what you're trying to do, your code, and any error messages. Peers and instructors can help you there.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">How do I book a 1:1 session with an instructor?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">Office hours details and scheduling instructions will be shared on Mattermost and Canvas. Look for announcements about available slots.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">I'm stuck on a lab — what should I do?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">First, re-read the lesson materials on Canvas. Then try searching for the error message online. If still stuck, post on Mattermost #module-help with details. Don't suffer in silence — everyone gets stuck, and asking for help is how you learn faster.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">I'm falling behind — who do I talk to?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">Reach out to any of your instructors directly on Mattermost or Canvas. We're here to help you succeed, not judge you. The earlier you let us know, the more we can do to help you catch up.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-category">Assignments & Grading</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">Where do I submit assignments?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">All assignments are submitted through Canvas. Some coding assignments will use CodeGrade, which you'll access directly from the Canvas assignment page.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">Can I submit late?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">Check the specific assignment policy on Canvas. In general, it's always better to submit on time even if incomplete. Reach out to your instructor if you're facing difficulties meeting a deadline.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">What are the capstone projects?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">At the end of the programme (Module 6), you'll complete 3 capstone projects that demonstrate your full skillset: a regression model, a supervised classification model, and an unsupervised classification model. Each project is framed around solving a real business problem.</div>
</div>
