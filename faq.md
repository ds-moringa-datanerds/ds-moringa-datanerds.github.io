<div class="faq-category">Programme & Schedule</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">How long is the programme?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">The part-time programme runs for 37 weeks across 6 modules that progressively build your data science skills from Python basics through advanced machine learning.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">What's the weekly time commitment?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">Plan for scheduled class sessions plus 5–10 hours of independent study, practice, and assignments per week.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">What if I miss a class?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">Attendance is tracked and matters for your progress. If you miss a session, check Canvas for materials and the Mattermost #announcements channel for the post-session summary. Reach out to an instructor if you need help catching up.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">How is the course assessed?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">Module Labs (25%), Module Quizzes (5%), Summative Labs (45%), and Summative Quizzes (25%). There are also 3 capstone projects at the end of the course.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">How do I join the class sessions?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">All sessions are via Google Meet. Use the calendar invite sent to your email to join. If you haven't received an invite, contact your instructor on Mattermost.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-category">Technical Setup</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">What laptop specs do I need?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">Minimum: Core i5 processor or above, 8GB RAM, 500GB storage, and stable internet access.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">How do I install Python?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">We recommend Anaconda, which bundles Python with all the data science libraries you'll need. See the <a href="/resources/">Resources page</a> for step-by-step instructions.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">Do I need prior programming experience?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">No. Module 1 starts from the very basics of programming. What you need is curiosity, willingness to practice, and comfort with basic math and logic.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">How do I connect to Mattermost?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">Visit <a href="https://baraza.moringaschool.com/login">baraza.moringaschool.com</a>, create your account, then download the desktop or mobile app. Set the server URL to <code>https://baraza.moringaschool.com</code> and display name to <code>moringa-classroom</code>. See the <a href="/resources/">Resources page</a> for full instructions.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-category">Getting Help</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">Where do I ask technical questions?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">Post in <strong>Mattermost #module-help</strong>. Include what you're trying to do, your code, and any error messages.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">I'm stuck on a lab — what should I do?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">First, re-read the lesson materials on Canvas. Then search for the error message online. If still stuck, post on Mattermost #module-help with details. Don't suffer in silence — everyone gets stuck, and asking for help is how you learn faster.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">I'm falling behind — who do I talk to?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">Reach out to any instructor directly on Mattermost or Canvas. The earlier you let us know, the more we can help you catch up.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-category">Assignments & Grading</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">Where do I submit assignments?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">All assignments are submitted through Canvas.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">Can I submit late?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">Check the specific assignment policy on Canvas. It's always better to submit on time even if incomplete. Reach out if you're facing difficulties meeting a deadline.</div>
</div>

<div class="faq-item" onclick="this.classList.toggle('open')">
  <div class="faq-question">What are the capstone projects?</div>
  <div class="faq-answer">In Module 6, you'll complete 3 projects: a regression model, a supervised classification model, and an unsupervised classification model — each framed around solving a real business problem.</div>
</div>
